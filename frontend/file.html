New to UFC? No worries!

Here's what you need to know

the world's largest and most prestigious mixed martial arts (MMA) organization, known for hosting high-profile events where elite fighters from diverse martial arts backgrounds compete. Founded in 1993, the UFC initially aimed to determine the most effective martial art by pitting fighters of various disciplines—like boxing, Brazilian jiu-jitsu, wrestling, karate, and Muay Thai—against each other. Over time, UFC fighters evolved to become well-rounded martial artists, combining techniques from multiple disciplines.
